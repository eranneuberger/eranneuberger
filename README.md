# Product Leadership Training & Workshops

## About

I'm a product executive with 12+ years of experience building and scaling product organizations across SaaS, AI, and platform companies. I've led product teams from 0 to 20+ people, launched category-defining platforms serving 80+ enterprise brands with 20M+ monthly invocations, and successfully transformed organizations from ad-hoc development to product-led growth.

Now, I'm sharing these hard-won lessons through targeted training and workshops designed for product leaders, executives, and teams looking to elevate their product practice.

## What Makes These Programs Different

### Experience-Based, Not Theoretical
Every framework, story, and recommendation comes from actual product leadership experience - from scaling FaaS platforms to 80+ enterprise clients, transforming sales-driven organizations into product-led companies, and successfully embedding AI across products and processes.

### Focused on Structure & Culture
While many trainings focus solely on PM craft skills, these programs address the organizational structures, cultural patterns, and leadership approaches that enable product excellence. Because the biggest challenges aren't usually about techniques - they're about how teams are structured and how decisions get made.

### Action-Oriented
Participants leave with concrete 90-day action plans, experiment designs, and frameworks they can immediately apply. No abstract theory - just practical tools and approaches that work.

### Balanced Perspective
Drawing from experience at both fast-moving startups and global enterprises, these programs provide balanced guidance that acknowledges real-world constraints while pushing for excellence.
