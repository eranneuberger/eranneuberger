# Training & Lecture Themes Catalog

## Strategic Product Leadership

### "From Chaos to Product-Led Growth"
How to transition organizations from ad-hoc, sales-driven development to structured product practices. Draw directly from real transformation experience as CPO.

### "Building Product Teams from Zero"
Practical guidance on hiring, structuring, and mentoring product teams. Cover experience scaling from 0→20+ people and coaching first-time PMs.

### "The CPO Playbook: First 90 Days"
What incoming product leaders should focus on: assessing team capabilities, establishing credibility with stakeholders, and setting strategic direction.

---

## AI Integration & Modern Product Practices

### "AI in Product: Beyond the Hype"
Practical frameworks for integrating AI into products and internal workflows. Cover prototyping, discovery research, content generation, and workflow automation using real examples.

### "Building AI-First Features Users Actually Want"
How to validate AI use cases, avoid solution-seeking problems, and measure AI feature success.

---

## Platform & API Product Management

### "Platform Product Management Masterclass"
Leveraging FaaS platform experience (0→80 brands, 20M invocations) at a global enterprise. Cover developer experience, API design, ecosystem thinking, and platform metrics.

### "Developer-Focused Products: APIs, Documentation & DevEx"
Drawing from experience building messaging APIs and developer platforms at enterprise scale. How to build products developers love.

---

## Execution & Team Excellence

### "Product Discovery That Actually Works"
Continuous discovery practices, user interview techniques, rapid validation methods. Include camping platform MVP research approach.

### "OKRs, Roadmaps & Prioritization Frameworks"
Moving beyond feature factories to outcome-driven planning. Practical frameworks used at scale.

### "Cross-Functional Leadership Without Authority"
How PMs align engineering, design, sales, and executives around shared goals.

---

## Specialized Topics

### "Technical Replatforming: A Product Leader's Guide"
Managing large-scale technical transitions while maintaining product velocity (95% vendor dependency reduction story).

### "Product Metrics That Matter"
Moving beyond vanity metrics to actionable product analytics and experimentation frameworks.

### "Enterprise Product Management"
Unique challenges of B2B enterprise products: long sales cycles, complex stakeholders, custom requirements vs. platform thinking.

---

## Building Product-Centric Organizations

### "Designing Org Structures That Enable Product Excellence"
How to structure teams (feature teams vs. platform teams vs. customer journey teams), reporting lines, and decision-making authority. Cover when to centralize vs. decentralize product functions.

### "The Product Triad: PM, Design & Engineering Partnership Models"
Establishing healthy working relationships between product, design, and engineering. Different models (embedded, centralized, hybrid) and when each works best.

### "From Sales-Driven to Product-Led: Organizational Transformation"
Real transformation playbook from CPO experience: changing incentive structures, communication patterns, decision-making processes, and cultural norms. How to manage resistance and build coalitions.

---

## Governance & Decision-Making

### "Product Governance Frameworks That Don't Kill Innovation"
Balancing autonomy with alignment. Stage-gate processes, investment committees, escalation paths. How to establish decision-making frameworks at enterprise scale.

### "Stakeholder Management at Scale"
Managing competing priorities across sales, marketing, customer success, engineering leadership, and executives. Communication cadences, artifact sharing (roadmaps, status reports), and saying "no" gracefully.

### "Building a Product Council: When and How"
When organizations need centralized product leadership forums, who should participate, and how to make them effective vs. bureaucratic.

---

## Process & Operations

### "The Product Operating System"
End-to-end product operations: discovery processes, delivery workflows, quality gates, release management, post-launch reviews. What infrastructure product teams need to be autonomous.

### "Rituals That Scale: Product Team Ceremonies Beyond Standups"
Effective meeting structures: roadmap reviews, product critiques, quarterly planning, retrospectives, knowledge sharing sessions. Practices from leading 5 scrum teams at enterprise scale.

### "Building Product Ops: When and What to Centralize"
When to create dedicated product operations roles. What they should own (tools, data, training, process standardization) vs. what belongs with individual PMs.

---

## Culture & Capability Building

### "Creating a Culture of Continuous Discovery"
Embedding user research, experimentation, and learning into daily practice. Breaking the "we already know what users want" mindset.

### "Building Product Intuition Across the Organization"
Training sales, CS, and executives to think like product people. Shared language, exposure to customers, involving them in discovery.

### "The PM Career Ladder: Levels, Expectations & Growth Paths"
Defining what distinguishes Associate PM from Senior PM from Principal PM. Experience mentoring first-time PMs and scaling teams.

### "Product Team Health: Metrics Beyond Velocity"
How to measure whether your product organization is thriving: quality of discovery, stakeholder satisfaction, team engagement, strategic impact.

---

## Cross-Functional Integration

### "Aligning Product, Sales & Marketing: The GTM Partnership"
Experience developing 3-year GTM strategy as CPO. How product informs positioning, how sales feedback shapes roadmap, managing expectation mismatches.

### "Product + Customer Success: Closing the Feedback Loop"
Creating systems where CS insights actually influence product decisions. Preventing the "feature request graveyard."

### "Engineering-Product Collaboration Models"
Tech debt management, build-vs-buy decisions, involving engineering in discovery, managing tension between innovation and stability.

---

## Measurement & Accountability

### "Product Team KPIs: What Leadership Should Actually Measure"
Beyond feature delivery. Outcome metrics, leading indicators of product health, balancing short-term vs. long-term value.

### "Building Data-Informed Product Cultures"
Making analytics accessible, teaching teams to design experiments, creating feedback loops. PowerBI/MicroStrategy experience.

### "The Product Review: Board-Level Reporting That Drives Strategy"
Shareholder engagement experience. What executives need to see, how to present product strategy and progress credibly.

---

## Specialty Structures

### "Product Management in Startup vs. Scale-up vs. Enterprise"
How product practices evolve as companies grow. When to introduce processes, different organizational models for different stages.

### "Matrix Organizations: Making Product Work in Complex Structures"
Managing when PMs report to one leader but support multiple business units. Multi-regional experience at enterprise scale.

### "Platform Teams as Internal Products"
Treating internal teams as customers, platform team charters, balancing internal needs vs. external product velocity.
